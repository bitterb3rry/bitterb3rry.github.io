BlobBoi: the Game

Gameplay, Character & Narrative Design
April - May 2022

I was the visual and narrative designer for the BlobBoi game, a final game design made during my gameplay development course.

The game followed the story of one blobfish's adventure discovering the ancient art of gravity manipulation which the prologue gave context too. There were a total of 5 levels with increasingly diffuclt maps. The goal was to navigate through, rescue trapped fishes, without falling out to the unknown.

As the levels advance, shapes the maps bring forward narrative along with storytime advice given by messenger pearl would tell, as seen above. The messenger pearl was designed to standout, looking wise and feeling like a guide to the player.

The blobfish is the main character so it is the only sprite that is animated. When it is idle or sliding on a platform, its animations are eye blinking and tail wagging. Once it jumps into the air, it swims with its tail and its facial expression changes.
