Magic Hour

Interactive Website Design
December 2021

Magic Hour is a gallery designed as an interactive web page displaying 4 sunset photos I have taken over a period of time, as part of an assignment for Interactive Media course to make a site surrounding images. The intention of this project was to show off the vibrant colors created in nature during sunsets.

The design strategy was to be minimalistic and focused on typography and imagery. Attention is given to a balance between text and images to make sure the text doesnâ€™t over power the photos. The graphics are simple, highlighting the key colors and adding cohesion to the overall piece.

The main interaction feature on the implemented webpage is horizontal scrolling through the webpage. As the page is being scrolled through, the default blurry pictures will become clearer on hover, an interaction implemented as a result of feedback sessions.
