ASUCD Pantry Web App Redesign

UI Project
December 2021

The ASUCD Pantry is a free food bank service at UC Davis that provides food and daily necessities on campus for students and the Davis community. It is a store-like system where anyone could visit to take what they needed. When Covid hit, there was an urgent need for contact-less form of access to this service and thus a temporary ordering online was born. Fast forward to 2022, the pantry is needed more than ever and the previous temporary online systems were looking for a glow up in functionality and usability for the long run.

Having an online system made it easier and more efficient for both patrons of the pantry and the volunteers who run the organization, saving time and keeping everyone safe.

My role was to design a updated UI interface for the ordering process for pantry patron would use. To visualize the main function that I needed to implement, I created a task flow to show how a patron would place a pick-up order.
