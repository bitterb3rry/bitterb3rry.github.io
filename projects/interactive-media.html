Interactive Website Experiments

Interactive Media

While taking classes about interactive media, these are 2 of the projects that I enjoyed making the most.

Magic Hour

Magic Hour is a photo gallery featuring 4 sunset photos that I have taken. I experimented with horizontal scrolling and minimalism.



Blockbuster Videos

Blockbuster Videos is a project experimenting with video on websites. It features 3 random videos posed as channels with complementary subtitles.

Fun Fact: I had a ketchup video from a previous design project and I couldn't help but take the opportunity to use Dua Lipa's 'Levitating', my favorite song at the time, because of Da Baby's rap verse.
