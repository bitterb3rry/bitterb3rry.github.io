NEWS Real Estate Group

Designer
September 2021 - January 2022

NEWS was a new organization at UC Davis focused on real estate and finance topics. Social media played an important role in getting the organization noticed by the greater student population among all other existing organizations. Instagram posts allowed the group to have a hub for announcements and build an audience.

As part of recruitment, I designed business cards instead of flyers as a fresh and unique way to advertise for the group. The card was small and pocket sized and was handed out during involvment fairs. The team also had fun using the cards as prop a during the team photoshoot!
