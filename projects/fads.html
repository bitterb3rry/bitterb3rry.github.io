UC Davis 2022 Fashion Show

Website Design and Development
March 2022

As web designer in the Fashion and Design Society, I designed and developed the fashion website used as the information and ticketing portal to the fashion show for the annual UC Davis Picnic Day.

The site was designed to display large amounts of information and be responsive to any device as people may be viewing from different sized devices such as laptops, tablets, and phones.

As part of promotion, flyers were posted around the school campus with QR codes leading to the site. Using the same design as the flyer on the hero image helps interested guests that came through QR codes confirm that they are at the right place for the show.

The layout was broken into 3 main parts, separated by linear gradient bars echoing the hero image, with each part focusing on one set of information we would like guests to know. Overall typography choice was kept simple so various browsers and devices can display it. My personal favorite aspect are the big crystal graphics that are used to frame the sections and added visual interest to guide the user down the page.
