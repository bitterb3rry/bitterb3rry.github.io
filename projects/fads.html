UC Davis 2022 Fashion Show

Website Design and Development
March 2022

As web designer in the Fashion and Design Society, I designed and coded the fashion website used as the information and ticketing portal to the fashion show for the annual UC Davis Picnic Day.

The site was designed and coded to display large amounts of information and be responsive to any device as people may be viewing from different sized devices such as laptops, tablets, and phones.
