Hi, welcome to user testing for flavors of emotion!

This session is used for me to evaluate how potential visitors of the site are able to interact and understand. It is also for me to find areas of improvement in my design and code. In no way is it to test your abilities to do any tasks during this session. The current project is a prototype in progress for the final version.

Your 3 tasks are:

  1. Read through the opening.
  2. Submit a card and refresh the page to see it.
  3. View filtered responses for Sweet.

Enjoy!
